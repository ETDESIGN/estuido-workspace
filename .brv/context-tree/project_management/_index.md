# Project Spider: Supplier Sourcing Overview

Project Spider manages the sourcing of a Battery Charging Dock System for Etia, targeting an annual volume of 80 docks and 1,200 batteries. The project maintains a benchmark target of 500 RMB per dock.

## Strategic Framework
- Supplier Candidates: STW (95% match) and Goochain (85% match) are the primary entities identified for procurement.
- Negotiation Protocol: RFQ documentation must strictly omit internal milestones, specifically the April 15, 2026, prototype target, to preserve leverage.
- Operational Lifecycle: The sourcing process is structured as: Supplier Identification → Pricing Benchmarking → RFQ Issuance → Negotiation.

For granular supplier profiles, specific pricing datasets, and procurement metrics, refer to project_spider_supplier_sourcing.md.
