# Project Spider: Supplier Sourcing Overview

Project Spider focuses on sourcing a Battery Charging Dock System for Etia, with an annual volume of 80 docks and 1200 batteries. The current benchmark price is established at approximately 500 RMB per dock.

## Key Findings and Strategy
- Supplier Identification: STW (95% match) and Goochain (85% match) have been identified as primary candidates.
- Negotiation Strategy: To maintain leverage, RFQ documentation must exclude internal deadlines, specifically the April 15, 2026, prototype target.
- Process Flow: The sourcing lifecycle follows a sequence of Supplier Identification, Pricing Benchmarking, RFQ issuance, and final negotiation.

For comprehensive details on sourcing data, supplier profiles, and specific pricing benchmarks, refer to the full documentation in `project_spider_supplier_sourcing.md`.
