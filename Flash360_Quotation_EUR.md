### ✅ PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS SUMMARY

#### Charging Dock (8-slot)
- **Dimensions:** Custom design for 8 power banks
- **Charging Method:** Pogo pin contact (no USB ports)
- **Security:** Metal locking mechanism with anti-theft design
- **Indicators:** LED status lights per slot
- **Communication:** UART serial data output
- **Material:** ABS+PC fire-resistant housing
- **Input:** 12V DC
- **Output:** 5V / 1A per slot

- **Capacity:** 5000mAh (nominal)
