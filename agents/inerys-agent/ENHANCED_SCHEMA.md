# Enhanced Google Sheets Schema for Inerys Agent
# Much more detailed tracking for B2B sales

## Sheet 1: Companies (Master Company Data)
Columns A-T:
A. Company Name
B. Website
C. Industry/Niche
D. Company Size (employees)
E. Annual Revenue (estimate)
F. Headquarters Location
G. Year Founded
H. Products/Services
I. Key Clients
J. Competitors Known
K. Eco-Certification (GRS, rPET, etc.)
L. Packaging Needs (estimated)
M. Budget Range
N. Decision Maker Level
O. Best Time to Contact
P. Temperature (Warm/Cold)
Q. Status (New, Contacted, Negotiating, Closed, Lost)
R. First Contact Date
S. Last Contact Date
T. Notes

## Sheet 2: Contacts (People at Each Company)
Columns A-O:
A. Contact Name
B. Role/Title
C. Email
D. Phone
E. LinkedIn Profile
F. Company (reference to Sheet 1)
G. Decision Maker? (Yes/No)
H. Influence Level (1-10)
I. Communication Style (formal/casual/direct)
J. Best Contact Method
K. Last Contacted
L. Response Rate
M. Notes
N. DO NOT CONTACT flag
O. Verified Date

## Sheet 3: Conversations/Interactions
Columns A-P:
A. Date
B. Time
C. Company (reference)
D. Contact Person (reference)
E. Interaction Type (Email/Call/Meeting/WhatsApp)
F. Direction (Inbound/Outbound)
G. Subject/Topic
H. Email Sent? (Yes/No/Draft)
I. Response Received? (Yes/No/Pending)
J. Next Action Required
K. Next Action Date
L. Outcome
M. Follow-up Required? (Yes/No)
N. Follow-up Date
O. Conversation Notes
P. Internal Notes

## Sheet 4: Opportunities/Deals
Columns A-Q:
A. Opportunity ID
B. Company (reference)
C. Opportunity Name
D. Value (€)
E. Probability (%)
F. Stage (Prospecting/Qualification/Proposal/Negotiation/Closed)
G. Expected Close Date
H. Products Interested In
I. Quantity
J. Competitor Considered
K. Our USP Highlighted
L. Objections Raised
M. Created Date
N. Last Updated
O. Won/Lost Reason
P. Deal Notes
Q. Assigned To

## Sheet 5: Organizational Charts (Ornigram)
Columns A-Q:
A. Company (reference)
B. Parent Company
C. Subsidiaries
D. Department/Division
E. Contact Name
F. Role/Title
G. Reports To (contact reference)
H. Direct Reports (count)
I. Influence Level (1-10)
J. Decision Authority (1-10)
L. Budget Authority (Yes/No)
M. Technical Authority (Yes/No)
N. Notes
O. Last Verified
P. Source
Q. Verified By
