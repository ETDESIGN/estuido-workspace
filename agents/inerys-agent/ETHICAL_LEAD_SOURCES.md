## Ethical & Legal Reasons

### 1. Privacy Violations
- Scraping personal/business emails without consent = unethical
- People have a right to control their inbox
- GDPR (Europe) and other privacy laws protect this

### 2. Legal Issues
- **GDPR** (Europe): Illegal to collect emails without consent
- **CAN-SPAM Act** (US): Illegal to send commercial emails without permission
- **Spam Laws**: Most countries have anti-spam legislation
- **Fines**: Up to €20M or 4% of global revenue under GDPR

### 3. Email Deliverability
- Spam complaints = Florian's emails blocked
- Once blacklisted, hard to recover
- Professional reputation damaged

### 4. Business Ethics
- Cold outreach should be to legitimate prospects, not random emails
- Quality over quantity
- Real B2B requires research and personalization
