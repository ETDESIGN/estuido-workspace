# BUSINESS CONTEXT: INERYS & PROTEKE

## Company Overview

### Inerys (Founded 2010)
**Core Business**: High-end B2B manufacturing of custom bags, pouches, and promotional packaging.

**Unique Value Proposition (UVP)**:
- **End-to-End Control**: Design → Prototyping → Production → Logistics
- **Owned Factory**: Full control over China production facility
- **Supply Chain Transparency**: No black-box outsourcing. Florian's team owns the process.
- **Price Competitiveness**: Factory-direct pricing without middlemen
- **Quality Assurance**: Direct oversight of production standards

**Product Lines**:
- Custom cosmetic pouches and beauty packaging
- Branded promotional bags and tote bags
- Retail pouches for fashion, accessories, food
- Limited edition packaging and seasonal collections
- Corporate gift items and merchandise

### Proteke (Eco-Subsidiary)
**Mission**: Sustainable packaging solutions for environmentally-conscious brands.

**Eco-Credentials**:
- **GRS-Certified** (Global Recycled Standard): Certified recycled content tracking
- **rPET Products**: Recycled polyethylene terephthalate from post-consumer waste
- **Compostable Materials**: Plant-based, biodegradable packaging options
- **Natural Materials**: Organic cotton, jute, hemp blends
- **Closed-Loop Options**: Take-back programs for packaging recycling

## Typical Pain Points for Prospects

### Common Objections (How to Address)
- **"We already have a supplier"** → "Are you happy with their transparency and pricing? Many clients switch after discovering our direct factory model."
- **"China quality concerns"** → "We own our factory. I'll send you photos from our production floor. Our clients like L'Oréal don't compromise on quality."
- **"We need eco-friendly options"** → "Perfect. Our Proteke line is GRS-certified with full traceability. Let me send you our certification documents."
- **"Your MOQ is too high"** → "We can flex from 500 units for pilot orders. Would you like to start with a test batch?"
- **"Lead time is too long"** → "Standard is 6-8 weeks, but we have express options for urgent projects. When's your deadline?"
