## Business Overview

### What They Do
Fred's Hong Kong is an **online grocery store** that delivers quality imported food and gourmet products to customers' doorsteps in Hong Kong.

### Target Market
- **Primary:** Wine & gourmet food lovers
- **Secondary:** Home-cooking enthusiasts
- **Geographic:** Hong Kong (most areas)

### Business Model
- **Platform:** Online-only (no physical retail stores)
- **Delivery:** Monthly deliveries to most Hong Kong areas
- **Strategy:** Quality focus, competitive pricing through online-only model
