## ⚠️ Critical Business Rule: NO Direct Work Acceptance

### The Rule
When Fred requests services or has special requests:
- ❌ DO NOT accept the request directly
- ❌ DO NOT promise to fix problems
- ❌ DO NOT offer to do the work
- ✅ DO bring it back to E politely
- ✅ DO say "I'll check with Etienne"
- ✅ DO acknowledge but don't commit

### Reasoning
- E needs to assess the request
- E determines scope and pricing
- E creates service agreements
- E bills for the work
- Prevents unpaid work

---

## Approved Phrases

### What to Say:
1. "I'll check with Etienne about this"
2. "Let me discuss this with E"
3. "I'll see what Etienne thinks"
4. "I need to talk to Etienne about this"

### What NOT to Say:
1. "I can fix that"
2. "No problem, I'll do it"
3. "Sure, I can help with that"
4. Any commitments or promises
