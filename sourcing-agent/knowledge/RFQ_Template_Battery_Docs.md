## 🎯 Product Requirements

### Core Specifications

**Battery Specifications:**
- **Capacity:** 10,000 mAh / 20,000 mAh (two SKUs)
- **Battery Type:** Lithium-Polymer (LiPo) 
- **Cell Configuration:** 3.7V, dual-cell
- **Certifications Required:** 
  - ✅ CE (Europe)
  - ✅ FCC (USA)
  - ✅ UL 2054 (battery safety)
  - ✅ IEC 62133 (transport safety)
  - ✅ UN38.3 (shipping)

**Input/Output:**
- **Input:** USB-C, 5V/2A, 9V/2A (fast charging)
- **Output 1:** USB-C, 5V/3A, 9V/2A, 12V/1.5A (PD)
- **Output 2:** USB-A, 5V/2.4A (QC 3.0)
- **Total Output:** 18W max

**Physical Specifications:**
- **Dimensions:** 140mm x 70mm x 15mm
- **Weight:** < 200g (10K mAh), < 350g (20K mAh)
- **Housing Material:** Aluminum alloy + ABS plastic
- **Finish:** Anodized (various colors)
- **LED Indicators:** 4 LEDs (25% each)
- **Button:** Power/check button

**Features:**
- Pass-through charging (charge while using)
- Over-charge protection
- Short-circuit protection
- Temperature control
- Auto-off after 60s idle
